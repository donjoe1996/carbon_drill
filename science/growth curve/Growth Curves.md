1. What it is?
	- A mathematical model (s-shaped) that describe how trees grow from small to maximum size overtime, showing three patterns: a). cumulative growth b). growth rate c). growth acceleration
2. Why we need it for ARR:
	- To predict how much carbon can be sequestered by planting forest
