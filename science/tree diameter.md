**1. What it is:**

- The diameter of a tree trunk measured at exactly 1.3 meters above ground - a standardized, simple measurement you can take with a tape measure in under 30 seconds per tree

**2. Why we need it for ARR:**

- DBH is the "master key" that unlocks everything else - through [[allometric equations]], this single measurement predicts tree height, total [[biomass]], carbon content, and feeds directly into growth curves, making it possible to calculate carbon stocks across thousands of trees without cutting them down or climbing them
